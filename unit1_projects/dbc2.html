Tiramisu Recipe

A delicious Italian dessert!

Ingredients

3 eggs, separated
1/2 cup sugar
1 8-oz tub mascarpone
2 tablespoons rum
3 oz dark chocolate bar
1 package of 24 ladyfingers
1/3 cup espresso

Preparation

Cream

1. Using two bowls, separate egg yolks from whites.

2. Whip egg white with egg beaters until stiff. Peaks should form when dipping a utensil into the mixture. Set aside.

3. In a separate bowl, mix yolks and sugar until light in color. Then add mascarpone and rum.

4. Gently fold egg whites into mixture. Set aside.

Assembling

5. Lay out lady fingers on cookie sheets or aluminum foil.

6. Drizzle espresso evenly over ladyfingers using a spoon.Ladyfingers will absorb the liquid and become dark in color.

7. In a small glass dish, layer 8 ladyfingers next to one another, making them as compact as possible.

8. Layer about one third of the cream on this top layer.

9. Using a lemon zester, grind chocolate bar over the cream to make an even layer of chocolate flakes.

10. Repeat layering ladyfingers, cream, and chocolate two more times. Chill for several hours.

About

This recipe is an easy-to-make, beautiful dessert with no baking required! It contains chocolate, coffee, and rum. The cream is light and fluffy with soft, espresso-drenched lady fingers in between. Do it. Do it now.
